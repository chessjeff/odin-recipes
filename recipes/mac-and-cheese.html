Mac and Cheese

Beth's Mac and Cheese Bake recipe that is really good

Ingredients

  • 1 box pasta
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 4 tablespoon butter
  • 4 tablespoon flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 5 cups milk
  • 6 cups shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Cook macaroni and set aside
  2. cook onion in butter until brown/tender
  3. stir in flour and pepper
  4. add milk all at once
  5. cook over medium heat until thick and bubbly
  6. add cheese and melt
  7. stir in macaoni and transfer to casserole dish
  8. bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 25-30 minutes
  9. let stand 10 minutes
